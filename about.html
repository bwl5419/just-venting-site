About Just Venting

Meet Keith Stewart

West Milford local. 30-year volunteer firefighter. The person who shows up every time you book.

Keith Stewart.
Owner · Just Venting LLC · Volunteer Firefighter

Keith Stewart has lived in West Milford his whole life and has been a volunteer firefighter for over 30 years. In that time, he has responded to more house fires than most people will ever see up close, and he knows exactly what causes them. Lint-clogged dryer vents are one of the most common culprits, and almost every one of those fires was preventable.

In 2023, Keith founded Just Venting LLC to do something about it. He wanted to bring that fire safety knowledge out of the firehouse and into the homes of his neighbors. The goal was simple: give Northern NJ homeowners a reliable, honest, and genuinely expert option for dryer vent cleaning, run by someone who actually understands the risk.

Keith does every job himself. When you call Just Venting, you are talking to Keith. When someone shows up at your door, it is Keith. He is meticulous about his work, takes time to explain what he found and why it matters, and does not leave until everything is right. If something ever goes wrong, he makes it right, no questions asked.
